At Sinha Academy, we believe in making language learning an enjoyable and interactive experience for young minds. Our curriculum is tailored specifically for children aged 6-10, ensuring they grasp the fundamentals of Hindi in a fun and engaging manner.

Our Curriculum

We cover essential Hindi language concepts that form the foundation of fluency and literacy. Our lessons include:

Introduction to Hindi vowels and their sounds.

Understanding the vowel signs that modify the sounds of consonants.

Learning the nasal sound represented by the dot above a letter.

Grasping the nasalized sounds in Hindi

Exploring the voiceless glottal fricative represented by ":"

Mastering the combined consonants.

Learning about the conjunct consonants

Detailed study of vowel signs and their combinations.

Understanding the complete set of consonant-vowel combinations
